Publishing Your Project

One of the best parts of Vizor is sharing the worlds you build with your friends.

Publishing

To share your creation, you first need to publish your project.

Do It Yourself!
  • Click the “Publish” button on the top right of Vizor’s editor.
  • Give your project a name.
  • Make your project public.
  • Hit the publish button!
Here’s a video walk-through of the steps above.

When you complete the above, you’ll be taken to a website with the URL of vizor.io/(your user name)/(your project name). If your user name is “techstart” and your project name is “TheEmptyDesert” the URL would be vizor.io/techstart/TheEmptyDesert.

Warning: Capitalization matters when typing Vizor user and project names.

You can view, edit and delete your projects at vizor.io/(your username).

Viewing

Although you can build Vizor projects on any computer, you’ll need a computer or phone with WebVR to view Vizor projects in VR. You can learn more about installation on TechStart’s Installing WebVR guide.

View Your Project!
  • Open Firefox Nightly on your computer.
  • Enter the URL of your published project.
  • Click on the screen, and icons should appear near the bottom.
  • Click the “Enter VR” button (represented by a binoculars icon) and put on your headset to try your project in VR!

    • Here’s a video walk-through of the steps above.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You’ve completed your first VR project. Now’s a great time to review what you learned:

  • How computer graphics and VR can be used to create digital experiences.
  • How to use a platform named Vizor to build a virtual world you can share with anyone.

Stay tuned for more projects from TechStart. We’ll soon add projects that let you use code to create interactive and more customized worlds! Don’t want to wait? Check out video and written tutorials built by the Vizor team.
