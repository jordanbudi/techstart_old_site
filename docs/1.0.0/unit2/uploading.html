Uploading Your Image

Time to view your 360 images!

Viewing Your 360 Worlds

Try It Yourself!
    First, we need to copy the images from the camera to your computer.
  • Connect the Ricoh Theta S to the Techstart VR computer via the included USB cable.
  • Browse to the RICOH THETA S camera device through the Windows File Explorer.
  • Double click on the “RICOH THETA S” folder.
  • Double click on “Fixed Storage” folder inside of the “RICOH THETA S” folder.
  • Double click on “DCIM” folder inside of the “RICOH THETA S” folder. The folder contains all images stored on the camera.
  • Transfer all the camera images to your computer.

If you’re having trouble finding the camera device, with your teacher’s permission you can try downloading the camera software to make the process easier.

Now, let’s view the images!

Try It Yourself!
For each image you want to view, complete the following on the TechStart VR computer.
  • Open the Vizor 360 image viewer.
  • Drag and drop the image you want to view into the window.
  • Click “Enter VR” and put on the Oculus Rift.

How did the three images of different heights look and feel? After viewing them in an Oculus Rift, you should have a better idea of how scale works in virtual reality.
